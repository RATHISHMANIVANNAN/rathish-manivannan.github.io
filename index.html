About

I am currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Internet of Things) at Shiv Nadar University Chennai (2022–2026), and enrolled in the B.S Data Science and Applications program at IIT Madras (2022–2026) — having completed the Diploma in Data Science in 2024. My research blends Machine Learning, IoT, and Cybersecurity to design robust, resource-aware learning systems for distributed and embedded environments.

Research Statement

My research aims to develop adaptive and secure machine learning methods for distributed embedded systems. I focus on anomaly detection and defense mechanisms for IoT networks, privacy-preserving federated learning for resource-constrained devices, and resilient ML pipelines for cyber-physical systems. By integrating empirical prototyping on ESP32 and Arduino platforms with statistical anomaly detection techniques, I bridge theory and deployable solutions. I am also engaged in interdisciplinary research combining AI, computer vision, and IoT to design intelligent, efficient, and secure real-world systems.
